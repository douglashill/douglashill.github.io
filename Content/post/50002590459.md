[DuckDuckGo](https://duckduckgo.com/) will perform URL encoding and decoding. For example:
‘[url encode $](https://duckduckgo.com/?q=url+encode+%24)’ and ‘[url decode %23](https://duckduckgo.com/?q=url+decode+%2523)’. Handy.
