This [trick from Cult of Mac to disable iOS 26 update nags](https://www.cultofmac.com/how-to/stop-ios-update) seems to be working well on my iPad running iOS 18: *Enable iOS 18 Developer Betas*. Suddenly the OS is “up to date”. No more red badges or notifications that can’t otherwise be turned off.
