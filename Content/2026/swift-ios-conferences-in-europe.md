Here’s a roundup of upcoming Swift/iOS conferences in Europe. UK conference scene is strong! Ticket are available now for all of these except Do iOS.

- 4–6 May: [iOSKonf](https://www.ioskonf.mk/) in Skopje.
- 19–20 May: [Swift Craft](https://swiftcraft.uk/) in Folkestone.
- 3–4 June: [mDevCamp](https://mdevcamp.eu/) in Prague.
- 7–10 Sept: [iOSDevUK](https://www.iosdevuk.com/) in Aberystwyth. Talk submissions just closed. I’m planning to be there.
- 13–14 Oct: [SwiftLeeds](https://swiftleeds.co.uk/) in Leeds. [Talk submissions](https://swiftleeds.co.uk/cfp) will open on 2 March.
- 11–12 Nov: [Do iOS](https://do-ios.com/) in Amsterdam. Talk submissions aren’t open yet.

Last year was the final Pragma. No annoucements yet from Swift Connection, Server-Side Swift, or NSSpain.

Let me know if I missed one.
